WHO WE ARE

Blue Nation is a part of Nolyx Society, a community of innovators, builders, and visionaries united by our passion for blockchain technology and decentralized systems. We believe in creating a more equitable digital future through collaboration and shared knowledge.

Our members come from diverse backgrounds including development, design, business, and more. but we're all committed to advancing the Web3 ecosystem in Indonesia and beyond.
